ESPRESSO CAFE

Espresso brings out the best and the worst in coffee.
Those who experienced a god-shot will forever be searching for the next god-shot.
Those who have tasted a sink-shot on the first try will shudder at the thought of it.

Seven Factors of Good Espresso

  • Freshly Roasted & High-Quality Arabica Beans
  • Great Aroma
  • Grind Size
  • Dose(Grind Volume)
  • Tamp
  • Water Temperature
  • Brew Time & Yield
